Motoplast

Moto-plast is the largest stretch and thermo film producer in Serbia, with a market that extends to the Balkans, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Denmark, Russia, and more. For an industry that typically does not have a strong presence on social networks, it was a challenge but also a pleasure, to create an adequate strategy for an industry giant like Moto-plast.

We took a slightly different strategy to the launch campaign and the start of our social media presence than the rest of the competition. Almost every part of the corporation was the target of content development, including a wide range of products, production plants, working environment, big investments, and renewable energy.
