Illy Serbia

We were ecstatic when the world's leading coffee manufacturer and distributor approached us with the responsibility of developing a Social Media strategy and presence for Illy in Serbia. It was both a thrill and a challenge to have the opportunity to develop such a well-known brand on a new marketing and social media platform.

The goal was to make the content engaging and entertaining while also being instructive, all while showing what Illy Serbia has to offer. The aim is to secure the audience's attention and turn them into full-time customers.

Our content was developed using market segmentation and a comprehensive appeal strategy which included emotion, education, brand value, and social activation. We were successful in implementing a personalized, detail-oriented approach with the overarching plan by engaging with followers on a regular basis.
