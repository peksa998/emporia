Web development

Here at Emporia marketing agency, our priority is your online presence. Social media is a tremendous and crucial communication tool, but if it isn't directing traffic back to your website, you're doing something wrong.

The primary task that our design and development team has is to envision, design, and develop cutting-edge websites and applications.

Whether you need an existing website rebuilt or a website or application created for a new startup or business you just started, our unique blend of expertise, which includes digital marketing, design, development, media production, and branding, is a perfect match. Below is a list of the primary services we provide, along with a brief description.

Web design

As of 2018, mobile traffic accounted for more than 61 percent of all internet traffic. You're doing something wrong if your website isn't entirely responsive on mobile devices.

Whether your website is a presentation or an online store, you should invest in comprehensive responsive design across all devices to ensure maximum customer happiness and an ideal experience.

Our ultimate goal is to design your website to look great on any device.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Most of the standard internet users have one thing in common when they turn on their computer â€“ They search for something on Google. The technique of boosting the quality and quantity of search engine traffic to a website or a web page is known as search engine optimization (SEO).

Our web development team's primary responsibility is to use this technique on your website while developing or re-designing it.

Sales pages

It's never simple to create a landing page that assures optimum user conversion, lead generation, and sales. You must understand what you're doing.

The process of creating landing pages includes composing the best possible language and constructing the best possible sales funnel that motivates the user to do precisely what you want. As an agency, we have gathered a lot of experience building conversion-focused landing pages.

Application design

Mobile applications are leading the way in the era of mobile-first experiences.

After listening to your concept, we have a thorough discussion, and last, we allow our marketing and design teams to create the right blend of interface and experience in order to create the best app.

Performance analysis

Do you believe your website is functioning poorly? Perhaps your sales page has a low conversion rate, or your buyers are misunderstanding the message.

We evaluate your website, customer experience, and user interface to develop a strategy for increasing sales and conversions by improving your online platform.

Web development

Web development, of course, is what pulls all of our above-mentioned web design work together and gives life to a project.

Our development team has experience building a wide range of websites, from simple landing pages for businesses, entrepreneurs, startups, and restaurants to large online stores for global brands.
